About Me

Robin Mukherjee

Personal & Corporate Coach

My life coaching approach is considered warm and respectful. Empowering the client with compassion is a key driver in my technique. Being trained rigorously in Coaching principles, I augment my sessions with NLP and Hypnosis techniques. My coaching relationship rests on Emotional Safety. A safe emotional sanctum inspires creative, ( and even daring) breakthrough solutions.

I am result-oriented. My goal is to create positive change in your life, or your organization, quickly with a keen eye on respect for your environment. I will use strategies that were established over the last 40 years. Your lasting success is important to me.
